# CANON_OPERATORS

## Latent operators

### Enable
- increases Omega_V
- reduces dissipation baseline

### Express
- increases T
- activates Gamma
- produces observable dynamics

### Regulate
- increases Pi
- reduces Gamma
- stabilizes system

### Constrain
- enforces Pi_K
- removes invalid states
- defines collapse boundary

---

## Observation operators

### P (projection)
- reduces full state to observable Y
- introduces L_P

### Probe
- samples system
- generates traces Z

### Filter
- removes noise
- may distort signal

### Render
- creates views
- does not alter state

### Calibrate
- adjusts observation fidelity

---

## Critical rule

Observation operators:
- do not govern system behavior
- do not modify latent state

They only reveal it (imperfectly).
